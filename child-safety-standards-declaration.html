Sipn Bourbon – Child Safety & CSAE Prevention Policy

At Sipn Bourbon, we are firmly committed to ensuring the safety and protection of all users on our platform — especially children. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy toward any form of Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) and strictly prohibit the use of our app for such activities.

Our Commitment to Child Safety

Sipn Bourbon does not permit the presence, promotion, or sharing of content that exploits or endangers children in any way. We take proactive measures to detect, prevent, and report CSAE-related content or behavior.

Prohibited Activities

The following activities are strictly prohibited on our platform:

  • Uploading, sharing, or viewing any form of CSAE content
  • Grooming, solicitation, or any form of inappropriate communication with minors
  • Attempting to use the platform to exploit or endanger children

Violations of these policies will result in immediate account suspension or removal, and may be reported to law enforcement where applicable.

Content Moderation and Detection

To maintain a safe and secure environment, we utilize:

  • Automated tools to scan for suspicious media and activity
  • Manual review of flagged content by trained moderators
  • Regular audits to ensure compliance with child safety standards

Our moderation tools and processes are regularly updated to reflect industry best practices in child protection.

User Reporting

We encourage users to take part in maintaining a safe community. Suspicious behavior or content can be reported via:

  • Email: social@sipnbourbon.com
  • In-app reporting features (coming soon)

All reports are investigated promptly and confidentially, and appropriate action is taken based on our findings.

Cooperation with Authorities

Sipn Bourbon fully cooperates with:

  • Local, national, and international law enforcement agencies
  • Child protection organizations
  • Efforts to combat CSAE globally

We report CSAE-related violations to the appropriate legal bodies, including the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) where applicable.

Ongoing Commitment

We are dedicated to continuously improving our safety policies, tools, and practices. Our mission is to provide a platform that is secure, respectful, and free from abuse.

If you have any questions or concerns about our Child Safety Policy, please contact us at: social@sipnbourbon.com
